The Coquette Style Guide

Step into the world of soft femininity, vintage romance, and effortless charm. Here, we’ll teach you the essentials of coquette fashion, from the must-have wardrobe pieces to the perfect accessories with example outfits.

Whether you're just discovering the aesthetic or looking to refine your style, we’ve got everything you need to embrace the coquette fashion style.

About

The coquette fashion aesthetic is all about soft, romantic, and feminine details with a hint of vintage glamour. It blends elements of old-money elegance, ballet core, and soft grunge, creating a look that feels both delicate and alluring.

Key colors in the coquette fashion aesthetic include soft pinks, pastel hues, whites, creams, and light lavender.
